Three myth-busters

Did you know new solutions don’t need to involve swapping out everything you have or be expensive? In this article, we’ll break down the three biggest myths about SD-WAN and show you how this insight allows you to connect different.

SD-WAN Myth 1: There is only one way to manage application performance over multiple paths – forward error correction (FEC).

FEC works fine, but it takes up bandwidth, up to 50% of it.

If you are using 2 x 100Mbps links, up to 100Mbps is being used to help manage your SDWAN solution. So you are paying twice the price for bandwidth.
